# aish — Architecture

aish's architecture is documented alongside its goals in [GOALS.md](./GOALS.md).
Split rationale: vision and engineering are tightly coupled for a shell at this
stage; separating them caused more cross-references than insight.

Ecosystem and federation architecture (XDAO, the *-Atoms catalog standard) live
in their own repositories, not here:

- **[github.com/convergent-systems-co/xdao](https://github.com/convergent-systems-co/xdao)** — Ecosystem overview, federation layer, *-Atoms catalog directory, distributed-federation architecture.
- **[github.com/convergent-systems-co/atoms-spec](https://github.com/convergent-systems-co/atoms-spec)** — Atoms Catalog Standard. The canonical specification every `*-Atoms` catalog conforms to.
